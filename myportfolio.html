It’s all about helping students use what they already have, with recipe suggestions, skill-based options, and even a little waiting game for fun. The goal? Creative Pitch & Enactus Project

Here are my two pitches: Third Hand and The Food Loop.

The Third Hand Pitch

My classmates and I created Third Hand—a project that gives second-hand clothes a third chance. I was responsible for the layout and illustrations, which was a really fun experience to work on!

The Food Loop Mood Board

The Food Loop is an Enactus project that’s all about tackling food waste and helping those in need. We collect surplus food from local businesses and communities, and get it to people who could really use it. As the project leader, I’m working with an amazing team to spread the word and encourage volunteers to get involved.
